Phones

Android is very insecure, even de-googled phones, even F-Droid apps.
For proof check "permissions" for each app in F-Droid.
A middle ground is Ubuntu Touch and other less popular OS's.
In comparison in IOS; you can restrict/disable EVERY APP ACCESS at the OS level (compared to Android).

The best secure phone is a dumb phone (for calls only) and a separate secure Wi-Fi device,
one that you can control/configure completely, maybe a Linux device. More coming soon...
